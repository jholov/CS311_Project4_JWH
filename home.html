You will be introduced to the opera legends that have shape our musical history. Their works of art live on today and continued to be performed in theatres all around the world. You will explore Georges Bizet whos Viennese classical style mixed with French melodies produced some of the best romantic operas of all time. Wolfgang Mozart who was known for his clarity and balanced in his pieces that could evoke powerful works of art. Giacomo Puccini who excellently captures his characters emotional states while delivering glorious melodies. Giuseppe Verdi who depicts his characters struggles so accurately that you sympathize even with the antagonist of the story. Lastly, Richard Wagner who brought revolutionary ideas to his music that helped evolve the genre for years to come. Each one left their own mark on the industry and will forever be remembered.
